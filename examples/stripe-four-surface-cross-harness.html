How these scores are computed
  • Static discovery /100 — the higher of two docs-site audits: v0 (conventional-path checklist — llms.txt, OpenAPI, sitemap, OAuth discovery, …) and v2 (a docs-graph crawl scoring link-reachable surfaces). max(v0, v2).
  • Agent discovery /100 — the share of a run's scored Phase-0 signals that passed (reached the authoritative source · used a concrete create action · avoided a stale/wrong source · authenticated). Efficiency (hops) is reported but not scored.
  • Content quality /100 — a weighted score over the OpenAPI spec's per-endpoint "smells" (Hermes taxonomy): 100 minus weighted smell prevalence, so a clean spec ≈ 100.
  • Task success % — the share of tasks whose round-trip oracle passed: the agent creates/mutates a resource and the verifier independently reads it back and asserts a server-confirmed field.
